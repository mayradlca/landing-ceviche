Our Culture

Peruvain cuisine is the result of the nation's multicural history, a fusion of local and international flavors, rooted from indigenous traditions and influences from immigrant from continents such as Europe, Africa and Asia have resulted in the most exquisite mix of ingredients one could ever try.

Ceviche, a millenary and recognized dish of the southern american gastronomy is a symbol of transition and adaption; our restaurant aims to follow its path by maintaining its roots while adding ingredients from the local cuisine into it.
